The Destruction of Docker's Bay

The End​

It is often the case that fairy tales have a definitive beginning and end. The real world, however, seldom confines itself to such limitations. Our story, set as it is within the Forgotten Realms, thus begins at the end of another: The port-town of Dockers Bay, located along the Sword Coast just West of The Cloud Peaks, saw a curious collection of characters: from the typical humans making their way in the world, to the demigods that lived among them as adventurers.

Long had the city been protected by powerful magic, beyond even that of the adventurers who resided there. So, when cataclysm befell the land, it confounded beliefs and shattered the sense of safety that all had resided comfortably under. It had started quite simply: A comet in the skies above. Over time, the comet revealed an unnatural nature - mages felt pain in their heads, people and animals were prone to emotional outbursts, and magical energies became unpredictable. As time passed, these effects grew in gravity, and new, worse effects appeared. Shadow creatures crept around the area, stalking the night - and certain people and creatures started being targeted.

None of these events, however, hinted at what was to come. A group of some of the most powerful adventurers - not just in Dockers Bay, but indeed the world - set out to investigate the comet, determine its origin, and put a stop to its increasingly dangerous effects. What they found was nothing short of an incomprehensibly overwhelming presence, nearly killing them all simply by way of hearing its thoughts through one of them as a medium. As the intrepid heroes retreated back to safety, events only grew worse. The sun turned red, and a massive, imposing figure appeared, levitating in the skies above the bay's massive harbor.

And then it awoke. None who were present to witness it directly survived, but what happened in its wake was apparent for all to see: The massive magical crystal towers which had long protected the town exploded, their devastating eldritch energy wiping out most ordinary people in the area.

The town was engulfed in magical flames of purple and green, and the dark silhouette of the massive imposing figure stood silent and still in the sky above as devastation spread across the land. A few of the adventurers, at least, were quick to act: escaping by various methods of teleportation and fast-moving travel on foot. Most others were not so fortunate, and were consumed in the cataclysm.

Fleeing East, the surviving adventurers of Dockers Bay set up a makeshift camp settlement at the foot of The Cloud Peaks. With only their clothes and equipment on their backs, and very little in the way of food and supplies, they regrouped, with more survivors finding their way to the makeshift settlement. It was there that these fine adventurers rested - discussing plans regarding the founding of a new settlement; perhaps somewhere far away, or perhaps somewhere else along the coast.
