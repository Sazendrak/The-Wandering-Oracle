Allowed 2024 Rules Content

General Rules​

General:

  • 2024 character creation content, subclasses, classes, feats, items can be submitted via the Homebrew channel

Classes:

  • Any approved 2024 Class can be played with a 2014 subclass, unless otherwise stated in the wiki.
  • You can choose a 2024 Subclass on a 2014 Class, unless otherwise stated in the wiki.
  • 19th Level Epic Boon feats are replaced with ASI or a Feat
  • Once you chose the 2024 version of a Class you can't multiclass into it's 2014 version or choose the 2014 version in Paragon leveling, and vice versa
  • 2024 Classes can start with their starting gold instead of equipment

Feats:

  • If you take a 2024 feat, you can't take its 2014 version as a feat, and vice versa

You can enter this state a number of times equal to your wisdom modifier per long rest. The saving throw DC for the spell equals 8 + your Wisdom modifier + your proficiency bonus
