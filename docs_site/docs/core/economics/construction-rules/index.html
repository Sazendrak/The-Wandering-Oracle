Construction Rules

Building Rules​

Walls and floors are all considered to be 1 feet thick, a 1 foot by 1 foot by 1 foot cube of material is considered to be 1 unit.

How to Build a Structure​

Determine the floor plan of your building. For example, you wish to build a 20 by 20 shack made of stone brick, the walls are say 8 feet high and for ease of calculation the roof is flat, there are no extra internal walls and is just open.

If the floor is 2 feet thick, it will be 20 long x 20 wide x 2 thick = 800 units The ceiling is normal (1 feet thick) and is 400 units Walls are 8 feet high and 20 long x 8 high x 1 wide, and you have 4 of them, therefore they equate to 640 units The building is a total of 1840 units, and can now be priced and the time can be found accordingly.

Base Construction Time​

Base Time: Units/100/number of workers = # workweeks it takes to construct.

Workers​

Decide the wages you want to advertise to determine how many NPCs will work. A Craftmaster will roll only 1 time and that number is final.

Cost (Per NPC/Day)Roll for Workers
5 gp1d20 NPC Workers
10 gp1d15 + 5 NPC Workers
15 GP1d10 + 10 NPC Workers
20 GP1d5 + 15 NPC Workers

Note: You can do Craftmaster-approved roleplay to increase worker count, but what you roll is limited to what you can achieve without roleplay.

Notes:​

  • On Elysium, the limit is 20 workers
  • Limit is based off population, depending on where you want to build. Contact craftmasters for a different limit.
  • You must decide on the wage before rolling for results
  • If you have some kind of other material you plan on using, ping a @craftmaster
