Circle of the Verdant Stalker

Description​

Druids of the Circle of the Verdant Stalker embody the lethal stillness and precision of Chult’s jungles. They move like serpents through the undergrowth, striking with poison and vanishing into foliage.

Features:

  • 3rd Level - Circle Spells
  • 3rd Level - Venomous Marksman
  • 6th Level - Aspect of the Striking Viper
  • 10th Level - Primal Toxin Mastery
  • 14th Level - Deathbloom Aspect

3rd Level - Circle Spells​

Your eye for all things visually harmonious grants you access to some spells when you reach certain levels in this class, as shown on the Circle of Beauty Spells table.

Once you gain access to one of these spells, you always have it prepared, and it doesn't count against the number of spells you can prepare each day. If you gain access to a spell that doesn't appear on the druid spell list, the spell is nonetheless a druid spell for you.

Druid LevelSpell
3rdEnsnaring Strike, Poison Spray
5thPlant Growth, Spike Growth
7thGrasping Vine, Stoneskin
9thTree Stride, Cloudkill (reflavored as a deadly plant toxin cloud)

3rd Level - Venomous Marksman​

Your training centers on the precision and toxins of Chult’s hidden hunters.

  • Blowgun Proficiency. You gain proficiency with the blowgun. You can use your Wisdom modifier instead of Dexterity for blowgun attack and damage rolls.

  • Toxin-Enhanced Blowgun. Your blowgun darts deal greater piercing damage as you grow in mastery.

Druid LevelBlowgun Damage Die
3rd1d6
10th1d8
14th1d10
  • Poisoner’s Tools. You gain proficiency with the Poisoner’s Kit.
  • Tough to Toxin. You have resistance to poison damage and advantage on saving throws against being poisoned.
  • Jungle Stride. You ignore nonmagical plant-based difficult terrain.

6th Level - Aspect of the Striking Viper​

You channel primal ferocity into a deadly, focused trance.

Activation. As a bonus action, expend one use of your Wild Shape to enter this Aspect for 1 minute. You can’t activate it again until you finish a short or long rest.

While in this Aspect:

  • Your blowgun attacks deal an extra 1d8 poison damage.
  • When you take the attack action to attack with your blowgun, hit or miss, you can make another attack with the blowgun.
  • If your first blowgun attack each turn hits, the target must make a Constitution saving throw (DC = your spell save DC) or be Poisoned until the end of its next turn.
  • A creature that fails this saving throw also takes an additional 1d8 poison damage.

10th Level - Primal Toxin Mastery​

Your venoms pierce the resilience of even the hardiest foes.

  • Toxic Bypass. Your blowgun attacks and druid spells that deal poison damage treat immunity to poison damage as resistance instead.

  • Debilitating Barrage. When you hit a creature that is Poisoned, you can use your reaction to force it to make a Constitution saving throw (DC = your spell save DC). On a failed save, it is Blinded until the end of its next turn. You can use this reaction a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, regaining all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

14th Level - Deathbloom Aspect​

Your mastery of venom and motion refines your Aspect of the Striking Viper into the Deathbloom Aspect.

When you activate your Aspect of the Striking Viper, it gains the following additional benefits:

  • Deadly Venoms. Your extra poison damage increases to 1d10, and creatures make their Constitution saving throws against your poison with disadvantage.
  • Viper’s Retreat. Immediately after you hit a creature with a blowgun attack, you can move up to half your speed without provoking opportunity attacks from that creature.
  • Predator’s Cunning. While the Aspect is active, you can take the Hide or Disengage action as a bonus action. You can also attempt to Hide even when you are only partially obscured by natural terrain, foliage, or dim light.
  • Toxic Perfection. You are immune to poison damage and the poisoned condition.
