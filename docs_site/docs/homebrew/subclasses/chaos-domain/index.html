Chaos Domain

In the domain of chaos, where whims rule and order trembles, the chosen clerics of the Chaos Domain walk a path paved with unpredictability and capriciousness. Whether by the whispered machinations of trickster deities or the primal forces of wild gods, these clerics wield the very essence of chaos itself.

Chaos Domain Spells​

You gain domain spells at the cleric levels listed in the Chaos Domain Spells table. See the Divine Domain class feature for how domain spells work.

Cleric LevelSpells
1stChaos Bolt, Dissonant Whispers
3rdMirror Image, Enlarge/Reduce
5thHaste, Slow
7thConfusion, Polymorph
9thDominate Person, Modify Memory

Chaos Bonus Proficiencies​

When you choose this domain at 1st level, you gain proficiency with martial weapons.

Channel Divinity: Chaos Warp​

Starting at 2nd level, you can use your Channel Divinity to warp probability around you, causing unpredictable effects. As an action, you present your holy symbol and a number of creatures equal to your wisdom modifier you can see within 30 feet of you. Those creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw against your cleric spell save DC or be affected by one of the following effects rolled randomly for each target:

1d4Effect
1The target takes 2d10 psychic damage.
2The target is blinded until the end of its next turn.
3The target is charmed by you until the end of its next turn.
4The target is frightened until the end of its next turn.

Twisted Fate​

At 6th level, your connection to the chaos that drives your every action allows you to twist the threads of fate in the world around you. As a reaction when a creature within 30 feet of you excluding yourself fails a saving throw, or check or misses an attack roll, the triggering creature must reroll the d20 and use the higher roll.

You can then choose a different creature you can see within 30 feet range. The chosen creature has disadvantage on the next attack roll, ability check, or saving throw it makes within 1 minute.

You can use this reaction a number of times equal to your Wisdom Modifier (minimum of once) per long rest.

Divine Strike: Chaos Domain​

At 8th level, you gain the ability to infuse your weapon strikes with divine energy. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra 1d8 psychic damage to the target. When you reach 14th level, the extra damage increases to 2d8.

Empowered Warp​

At 17th level, when you use your Chaos Warp, rather than rolling 1d4, roll 2d4. Each target that fails the save is affected by both effects you rolled.

If both d4s land on the same number, the target makes the saving throw with disadvantage and the effect is enhanced as follows:

d4Effect
1The target takes 4d10 psychic damage and can't use reactions until the end of its next turn
2The Target is blinded and subtracts 1d6 from any attack roll or saving throw it makes until the end of its next turn.
3The target is under the effects of the confusion spell until the end of its next turn.
4The target is under the effects of the slow spell until the end of its next turn.
