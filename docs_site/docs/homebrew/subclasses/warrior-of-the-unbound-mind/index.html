Warrior of the Unbound Mind

Description​

Monks who follow the Warrior of the Unbound Mind manifest fragments of their consciousness as psionic projections. These spectral clones serve as both weapons and tools, allowing the monk to fight across multiple positions at once, confuse enemies, and strike with thought alone.

Features:

  • 3rd Level - Psionic Projection
  • 6th Level - Mental Anchor
  • 11th Level - Unbound Multiplicity
  • 17th Level - Ascendant Mind

3rd Level - Psionic Projection​

At 3rd Level you learn to manifest one psychic shadow that shares your mobility.

  • Summon Projection: As a Bonus Action summon one Projection (lasts 1 hour) in an unoccupied space within 30 ft, the projection is a Construct of Psionic energy.
    • Stats: AC equals your Focus Save DC. HP equals two Martial Arts dice + Wisdom Mod. It is vulnerable to force damage, but immune to all conditions.
    • Mobile Strike: Any unarmed strike you make can originate from your space or the Projection's space. Attacks originating from the Projection deal Psychic damage.
    • Projection Movement: The Projection shares your movement speed. You can move the Projection up to your speed, using your movement allowance. It must remain within 60 ft. of you.
  • Psionic Burst: When the Projection is destroyed, use a Reaction and 1 Focus Point. Creatures of your choice within 10 ft. make an Intelligence Save, taking 2 Martial Arts dice psychic damage on a failure, or half damage on a success.

6th Level - Mental Anchor​

Your Projection can now bind foes with psychic energy.

  • Psionic Grapple: Once per turn, when a Projection hits a creature with an unarmed strike, you can forgo the damage to attempt a psionic grapple. The target must succeed on an Intelligence saving throw or be grappled by psionic force. The DC for the saving throw and any escape attempts equals your Focus Point DC.
  • The Projection immediately dissipates when the grapple succeeds, and cannot trigger Psionic Burst.

11th Level - Unbound Multiplicity​

Your mind’s grasp over its projections expands, granting superior tactical options.

  • Dual Existence: You can maintain up to two Psionic Projections at the same time. You may use all features from either Projection.
  • Projection Tactics: When a creature is hit by your stunning strike and within 5 feet of both your projections, they have disadvantage on the saving throw to resist the stun

17th Level - Ascendant Mind​

You gain the power to lock down multiple foes and achieve psychic dominance.

  • Unbound Grip: Your Psionic Grapple no longer requires you to forgo damage on the attack, and it can target a creature of any size.
  • Domination Burst: When a creature fails its save against your Psionic Burst, the psychic damage increases to 3 rolls of your martial arts die, and they have disadvantage on all wisdom, intelligence and charisma saving throws and checks until the end of their next turn.
  • Relentless Command: While a creature is grappled by your Psionic Grapple, you can spend 1 Focus Point as a bonus action to move that creature up to 30 ft. This movement uses up to 30 feet of your own movement allowance.
