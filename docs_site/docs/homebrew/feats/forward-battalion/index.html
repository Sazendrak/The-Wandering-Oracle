Forward Battalion

Weapon (glaive), legendary

Attunement

Requires Attunement by a creature with a Strength score of 15 or higher

Description

This magic glaive has tattered remains of a banner from the base of its blade and coiled around its shaft. The color and symbol have long faded and forgotten but whose task still persists. This weapon feels heavier than it looks to the wielder. Some say the banner appears at moments when the glaive is swung, showing the vibrant colors it once had. You have a +2 bonus to attack rolls made with this weapon. You also gain the following abilities.

Strength in Numbers. You gain an additional +1 bonus to attack rolls for each friendly creature within 20 feet of you up to a maximum of +3(total bonus of +5). You also give a +1 bonus to attack rolls to friendly creatures within range.

Assisted Assault. While you have at least one friendly creature within 30 feet of you, you deal an extra 3d4 of the weapon's damage on hit. Alternatively, you can forgo the extra damage at the start of your turn to give one friendly creature an extra 3d4 weapon damage on one attack they make on their next turn.
