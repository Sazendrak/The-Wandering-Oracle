Thumper

Weapon (maul), legendary

Attunement

Requires Attunement

Description

You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit with a melee attack using this weapon, it deals an extra 1d6 damage. In addition, the weapon ignores resistance to bludgeoning damage.

Attack rolls made with this weapon can't be hindered with spells and magical effects that increase the target's Armor Class except for the Haste spell(i.e. A target won't benefit from the bonus to AC from a Shield spell or Mage Armor spell against this magic weapon's attack rolls) as well as ignoring the magic bonuses of magical items.

When your attack roll is a critical hit, the target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of its next turn.
