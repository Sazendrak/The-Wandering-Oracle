Black Cleaver

Black Cleaver

Weapon (battleaxe), very rare

Attunement: Requires Attunement

Description

You have a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

After you have made a successful weapon attack with this weapon, you gain an additional 10 feet movement speed which lasts until the end of that turn.

Additionally, you can also lower the defensive prowess of the one you are attacking. The battleaxe has 5 charges, and it regains all expended charges daily at dawn. When you attack with this weapon, you can use your reaction to expend a charge to lower the target's AC by 2, includes against the triggering attack, until the start of its next turn. You can only use feature once per turn.
