Augmenter

Description​

The Augmenter is a unique blend of artificer and psychic, merging technology with mental prowess. These specialists understand the psyche and wield their own bodies as tools for both combat and manipulation. Whether augmenting their limbs or delving into the minds of others, Augmenters are masters of self-enhancement

Features:

  • 3rd Level - Augmenter Spells
  • 3rd Level - Self Augmentation
  • 5th Level - Extra Attack
  • 9th Level - Mind Over Matter
  • 15th Level - Master Augmenter
  • Augmenter Infusions

3rd Level - Augmenter Spells​

You always have certain spells prepared after you reach particular levels in this class, as shown in the Augmenter Spells table. These spells count as artificer spells for you, but they don’t count against the number of artificer spells you prepare.

Augmenter Spells reflect your understanding of the psyche and allows you to influence the mind of others.

Augmenter Spells

3rd Level - Command, Dissonant Whispers

5th Level - Tasha’s Mind Whip, Crown of Madness

9th Level - Antagonize, Fast Friends

13th Level - Raulothim’s Psychic Lance, Compulsion

17th Level - Synaptic Static, Modify Memory

3rd Level - Self Augmentation​

Starting at 3rd level, you master the technique of augmenting your own body. Each of the following parts of your body can be infused using your artificer Infusions; Torso, Head, Legs. You also gain access to Augmenter Infusions, these infusions are known and does not count toward your total of known infusions. For the purposes of normal Infusions, the Torso is seen as Armor, head as a Helmet or any peripheral that is worn on the head, and legs are seen as boots.

You also learn how to augment your arms with special arm weapons. These can also be infused, and you can only have one arm enhancement active at a time. Whenever you finish a long rest you can swap arm enhancements.

Arm Enhancements​

These Enhancements count as weapons and infusions can be applied to them. Each enhancement grants the artificer an applicable fighting Style based on the installed enhancement.

Mantis Blade​

You gain retractable blade attachments on either of your arms

Properties: Finesse

Fighting Style: Dueling

Damage: 1d6 piercing

Special: The blade can be retracted or unsheathed as a bonus action, leaving the hand free to wield other items. When you score a critical hit with the mantis blade the target needs to make a constitution save against your Spell DC or start to bleed. When bleeding the target loses hp equal to your proficiency bonus at the start of each of its turn. It can make the save again at the end of each turn.

Monowire Attachment​

These fine wires get pulled from your wrists and are wielded like a whip.

Properties: Finesse, Reach

Fighting Style: Two-Weapon Fighting

Damage: 1d4 slashing

Special: The wires count as two separate weapons for the purpose of two weapon fighting, but only require a single infusion. The wires use Intelligence for its attack and damage modifiers.

Gorilla Arms​

Your Hands and forearms are augmented with heavier metals and mechanical support.

Properties: Heavy

Fighting Style: Unarmed Fighting

Damage: Unarmed Strike, Bludgeoning

Special: You make your attacks with the Gorilla Arms as unarmed strikes. You also have advantage on Strength( Athletics) checks to escape, attempt, maintain grapples.

Arm Launcher​

You can load bolts or sling bullets into your arm and fire them as projectiles.

Properties: Ranged (40/80), Reload (6)

Fighting Style: Archery

Damage: 1d8 piercing (bolts), bludgeoning (sling bullets)

Special: The Launcher leaves your hands open while firing. On a hit, the launcher does 1d4 force damage to the target, all creatures within 5ft of the target must make Dexterity save against your spell save DC or take 1d4 force damage.

5th Level - Extra Attack​

Beginning at 5th level, you can attack twice, instead of once, whenever you take the Attack action on your turn.

9th Level - Mind Over Matter​

At 9th level, your deep understanding of the mind allows you to manipulate the physical world with your thoughts. You gain the following abilities:

  • Mental Shield: You gain resistance to psychic damage. Additionally, when you take psychic damage, you can use your reaction to reflect half of that damage back to the attacker.
  • Psychic Insight: You can use your action to read the surface thoughts of a creature you can see within 30 feet. This effect lasts for 1 minute, and the target must make a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC. On a failed save, you can also implant a simple suggestion (as per the Suggestion spell) into their mind. This ability can be used once per short rest.

15th Level - Master Augmenter​

At 15th Level your ability to enhance your augmentations has reached unparalleled heights:

  • You can now add 2 infusions to any of your augmentations.
  • You are able to use master properties for your arm augmentations. Each arm augmentation has two possible properties. When you switch out your arm augmentation you can choose which master property to use.
    • Mantis Blades: Graze, Sap
    • Monowire: Nick, Slow
    • Gorilla Arms: Sap, Push
    • Arm Launcher: Push, Vex

Augmenter Infusions​

Subdermal armor - 3rd Level, Torso​

Prerequisite: 3rd Level Augmenter, Unarmored

Item: Torso Augmentation (requires Attunement)

Effect: Your skin is reinforced with a layer of protective material, making you more resilient to physical attacks. Subdermal Armor makes the wearer’s AC equal 10 + Constitution Modifier + Intelligence Modifier

Chameleon Skin - 3rd Level, Torso​

Prerequisite: 3rd Level Augmenter

Item: Torso Augmentation (requires Attunement)

Effect: You can use an action to blend into your surroundings, gaining advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks for 1 hour. This ability can be used once per short or long rest.

Instant Disguise - 3rd Level, Torso​

Prerequisite: 3rd Level Augmenter

Item: Torso Augmentation (requires Attunement)

Effect: A sophisticated system of micro-projectors and sensors enables you to alter your appearance at will. As a bonus action you can cast the Disguise self spell on yourself, without spending a spell slot. This ability can be used a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once) per long rest.

Enhanced Sight - 3rd Level, Head​

Prerequisite: 3rd Level Augmenter

Item: Head Augmentation

Effect: You gain proficiency in the perception skill, and can use Intelligence instead of Wisdom for Passive perception and perception checks

Cat Eyes - 3rd Level, Head​

Prerequisite: 3rd Level Augmenter

Item: Head Augmentation

Effect: You gain dark vision out to 60 feet, if you already have dark vision from another source, its range is extended by 60 feet.

Alluring Visage - 3rd Level, Head​

Prerequisite: 3rd Level Augmenter

Item: Head Augmentation

Effect: The infusion highlights your positive features and filters your words, making you sound and look more alluring than you are. You can add your Intelligence modifier to any charisma(persuasion) check you make against a single target.

Fortified Ankles - 3rd Level, Leg​

Prerequisite: 3rd Level Augmenter

Item: Leg Augmentation

Effect: You gain a +10 feet to your walking speed, and your high and long jump distances are doubled.

Lynx Paws - 3rd Level, Leg​

Prerequisite: 3rd Level Augmenter

Item: Leg Augmentation

Effect: Your footfalls are dampened allowing for silent movement. You gain advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. Damage taken from falling is halved.

Swift Step - 3rd Level, Leg​

Prerequisite: 3rd Level Augmenter

Item: Leg Augmentation.

Effect: You can take the dodge action as a bonus action. This ability can be used a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once) per long rest.

Blood Pump - 6th Level, Special​

Prerequisite: 6th Level Augmenter

Item: Special Augmentation (requires attunement)

Effect: After finishing a short rest you can load any magical potion into the pump. You can then as a reaction to taking damage, prior to taking damage or making an attack inject the potion to activate its effect. Once activated the potion is consumed.

Arcane Shielding - 6th Level, Special​

Prerequisite: 6th Level Augmenter

Item: Special Augmentation (requires attunement)

Effect: When you are hit by a melee attack, you can use your reaction to cast Shield without expending a spell slot. This ability can be used a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once) per long rest.

Healing Nanobots - 6th Level, Special​

Prerequisite: 6th Level Augmenter

Item: Special Augmentation (requires attunement)

Effect: As a bonus action, you can release nanobots that heal a creature you touch for 2d8 + your Intelligence modifier hit points. This ability can be used a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once) per long rest.

Psychic Blades - 10th Level, Arm​

Prerequisite: 10th Level Augmenter

Item: Mantis Blade

Effect: When you hit a creature with the Mantis Blades, you can choose to deal an additional 2d6 psychic damage. This ability can be used a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once) per long rest.

Entangling Wires - 10th Level, Arm​

Prerequisite: 10th Level Augmenter

Item: Monowire Attachment

Effect: When you hit a creature with the Monowire Attachment, you can choose to restrain the target. The target must make a Strength saving throw against your spell save DC or be restrained until the end of your next turn. This ability can be used a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once) per long rest.

Impact Fists - 10th Level, Arm​

Prerequisite: 10th Level Augmenter

Item: Gorilla Arms

Effect: When you hit a creature with an unarmed strike, you can choose to knock the target prone. The target must make a Strength saving throw against your spell save DC or be knocked prone. This ability can be used a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once) per long rest.

Targeting Reticle - 10th Level, Arm​

Prerequisite: 10th Level Augmenter

Item: Arm Launcher

Effect: You gain a +4 bonus to attack rolls with the Arm Launcher. Additionally, you can use your reaction to impose disadvantage on a ranged attack made against you.

Optical Camo - 14th Level, Torso​

Prerequisite: 14th Level Augmenter

Item: Torso Augmentation (requires attunement)

Effect: Advanced optical technology allows you to blend into your surroundings, becoming nearly invisible. As a bonus action you become invisible until the end of your next turn, or until you take damage from any source. This ability can be used a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once) per long rest.

Reactive Coil - 14th Level, Head​

Prerequisite: 14th Level Augmenter

Item: Head Augmentation (requires attunement)

Effect: When you are hit by a melee attack, you can use your reaction to deal 1d6 lightning damage to the attacker. This damage increases to 2d6 if the attacker is wearing metal armor.

Hydraulic Jumps - 14th Level, Leg​

Prerequisite: 14th Level Augmenter

Item: Leg Augmentation (requires attunement)

Effect: You can use a bonus action to jump up to 30 feet in any direction. If you land within 5 feet of an enemy, you can make a melee attack as part of the same bonus action.
