Champions Weapon

Weapon (Any simple or martial weapon), legendary

quest-able; uncraftable

Attunement

Requires Attunement

Description

This weapon carries the last breath and fighting spirit of old champions, buried with them in their tombs as they carry their greatest arms into the afterlife.

You have a +3 to attack and damage rolls made when using this weapon. While wielding this sword, the threshold required for you to score a critical hit with weapon attacks is reduced by 1.
