Survivalist's Wayfinder

Wondrous item, uncommon

Description

This handheld device has four crystaline buttons that are each associated with a different element. The device has 4 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. While holding the device, you can use an action to expend 1 of its charges and press a button. Each button causes the device to swivel and point like a compass to the nearest relevant location, as determined by the button, for 1 hour or until another button is pressed. If there's nothing for the device to point to, it spins in place for the duration or until it's within range of something to point to.

Air. If you're inside or underground, the device points to the nearest outdoor exit within 1 mile of you. If you're outside, it points in the direction of the nearest storm within 50 miles of you.

Earth. The device points to the nearest cave entrance that's Medium or larger and within 1 mile of you. If you're underground, it points in the direction of the nearest unpolished gemstone within 100 feet of you.

Fire. The device points to the nearest active campfire, firepit, forge, or hearth that's within 1 mile of you.

Water. The device points to the nearest source of fresh, drinkable water that's within 1 mile of you.
