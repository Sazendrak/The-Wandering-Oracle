Channel of Devastation

Devastation Channeler

Description​

The Channel of Devastation represents channelers who prefer focusing their energy in short, powerful bursts, often leading to disastrous results. Those who choose this channel work tirelessly to keep their energy concealed and suppressed, until they can let it all out in a violent burst.

Features

  • 1st Level - Armaments of Devastation
  • 1st level - Accelerant
  • 6th Level - Devastating Presence
  • 11th Level - Devastating Strikes
  • 11th Level - Extra Attack
  • 17th Level - Culmination of Devastation

1st Level - Armaments of Devastation​

When you pick this channel at 1st level, you gain proficiency in glaives, greataxes, greatswords, halberds, and pikes. You also gain proficiency in heavy armor and shields.

1st level - Accelerant​

At 1st level, you’ve learned how to create an accelerant with your devastating energy, overcharging your attacks. When you hit with a melee weapon attack, you can force the target to roll a strength saving throw against your spell save DC. On a failure, the target is knocked prone and suffers an additional effect depending on your imbuement. You may use this feature a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier. When you take a short or long rest, you regain all expended uses.

Accelerant Effect​
Damage TypeEffect
AcidThe target’s AC is reduced by 1 until the end of your next turn.
FireThe target takes additional fire damage equal to one roll of your Elemental Imbuement die.
ColdThe target’s speed is reduced by half of their maximum until the end of your next turn.
LightningYou may force one other creature of your choice within 10 feet of the target to take lightning damage equal to two rolls of your Elemental Imbuement die.
ThunderThe target is pushed back 15 feet.

6th Level - Devastating Presence​

At 6th level, your mere presence is recognized as a threat, inciting opponents to focus their aggression on you. Each creature of your choice that starts its turn within 30 feet of you and can see you must roll a Wisdom saving throw against to your spell save DC. On a failure, the creature has disadvantage on attacks that don’t target you until the end of that creature’s turn. If a creature succeeds on this saving throw, they are immune to this effect for the next 24 hours.

11th Level - Devastating Strikes​

Starting at 11th level, whenever you deal damage with your Elemental Imbuement or a channeler spell that shares a damage type with your chosen Elemental Imbuement, you ignore resistance to that damage type, and you may treat immunity to that damage type as resistance.

11th Level - Extra Attack​

Beginning at 11th level, you can attack twice, instead of once, whenever you take the attack action on your turn.

17th Level - Culmination of Devastation​

At 17th level, you’ve wrought elemental devastation everywhere you’d gone, and have found the perfect way to channel it. When you hit with a melee weapon attack, you can reroll that attack against any number of other targets within 30ft, hitting each target simultaneously. You may use this feature a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier. When you finish a long rest, you regain all expended uses.
