Marshwarden Bow

Marshwarden Bow

Weapon (any bow), Rare

Attunement

Requires attunement.

Description

This bow is made of twisted cypress roots and covered in long, stringy moss. The bowstring is always slick with water.

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon and nonmagical arrows fired from this bow deal an extra 1d6 acid or poison damage on a hit (your choice each time).

When you’re in a forest or swamp and are hidden from a creature, missing with an attack against it doesn’t reveal your position.

Wretched Burst. When you hit a target with an arrow fired from this bow, you can cause it to erupt in a putrid cloud centered on the target, duplicating the effect of the “Stinking Cloud” spell (save DC 15).

This version of the spell doesn’t require concentration. Once this property of the bow has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn or until it’s spent at least 1 hour submerged in naturally occurring swamp water.
