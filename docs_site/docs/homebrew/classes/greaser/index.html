Greaser

Description:​

Since the dawn of humanity, there has always been a frontier to conquer. Between the wastes of the east, the fires of the hells, and the vastness of the astral plane, there has always been those that dared to tread the road less traveled. Traversing each regional obstacle brings with it a unique solution— the hellfire engines of Avernus, the spelljammers of the astral sea, and the machinations inspired by the great inventor Kwalish, all formed to blaze a trail through every path they come across.

Through the use of arcane influence and artificing prowess, a new school of thought has arisen among artificers; why spend your days walking through the most hazardous conditions when you can drive, sail, or fly through them instead? Thus, under the influence of vehicles from all different planes, the most ingenious of traveling artificers got to work.

3rd Level - Bonus Proficiency​

When you adopt this specialization at 3rd level, you gain proficiency with smith’s tools. If you already have this proficiency, you gain proficiency with one other type of artisan’s tools of your choice. In addition, you gain proficiency in land and water vehicles

3rd Level - Greaser Spells​

Starting at 3rd level, you always have certain spells prepared after you reach particular levels in this class, as shown in the Greaser Spells table. These spells count as artificer spells for you, but they don’t count against the number of artificer spells you prepare.

Artificer Level Spells

3rd Level - Burning Hands, Fog Cloud

5th Level - Locate Object, Dust Devil

9th Level - Slow, Stinking Cloud

13th Level - Hallucinatory Terrain, Sickening Radiance

17th Level - Passwall, Rary's Telepathic Bond

3rd Level - Arcane Vehicle​

By 3rd level, you’ve successfully managed to create your first vehicle for traversal. At the end of a long rest, you may create a vehicle by choosing one of each of the following vehicle mod options below. The vehicle is a large magical object that can be mounted as if it were a creature, after which it acts as a controlled mount. It shares its turn with you, and can only take the dash and disengage actions. It is immune to psychic damage and uses your saving throw bonuses when applicable.

If the mending spell is cast on it, it regains 2d6 hit points. If it has been destroyed within the last hour, you can use your smith’s tools as an action to repair it, provided you are within 5 feet of it and you expend a spell slot of 1st level or higher. The vehicle returns to working order after 1 minute with half of its hit points restored.

At the end of a long rest, you can create a new arcane vehicle if you have your smith’s tools with you. If you already have an arcane vehicle from this feature, the first one is immediately destroyed. The vehicle is also destroyed if you die.

Vehicle Mods​

Each vehicle can only have one vehicle mod of each option listed below active at one time. At the end of a short rest, you can use your smith’s tools to replace one vehicle mod of your choosing with another eligible of the same category. If a vehicle mod has prerequisites, you must meet them to learn it. A level prerequisite refers to your level in this class.

Weapon | Prerequisite | Details

  • Glob Launcher | N/A | Ranged (60/120). The glob launcher deals 1d10 bludgeoning damage. A creature hit by this weapon has its movement speed reduced by 10ft. until the end of its turn.

Weapons mounted on your vehicle are simple weapons you are proficient in that can only be used by yourself while you are mounted. If the weapon requires an attack roll, you can add your Intelligence modifier, instead of your Strength or Dexterity, to the attack and damage rolls. Use your spell save DC for any saving throw related to your weapon. These weapons can be infused.

5th Level - Extra Attack​

Beginning at 5th level, you can attack twice, instead of once, whenever you take the Attack action on your turn. Additionally, attacks from your mounted weapons count as magical for the purposes of overcoming resistance and immunity to nonmagical attacks and damage.

9th Level - Enhanced Modifications​

At 9th level, you’re able to fit more modifications on your vehicle. You may now have two specialized modifications and two mounted weapons active at once.

15th Level - Overdrive​

At 15th level, you learn to overclock your vehicle to force it to perform past its peak temporarily. As a bonus action, you can send your vehicle into overdrive for one minute. Doing so doubles its movement speed, makes it immune to all damage, and allows you to make up to three attacks with your mounted weapon each turn. At the end of the minute, your vehicle is immediately destroyed. You must finish a long rest to use this feature again, unless you spend a spell slot of 5th level or higher to use it again.
