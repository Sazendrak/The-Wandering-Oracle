Bombardier

Description​

Where the Alchemist brings life, the Bombardier brings ruin. Bombardiers are masters of volatile reagents, crafting incendiary flasks, caustic vials, and toxic brews designed to engulf their foes in lingering devastation. On the battlefield, they are walking arsenals, hurling explosive concoctions that set the world ablaze.

Features:

  • 3rd Level - Tool Proficiency
  • 3rd Level - Bombardier Spells
  • 3rd Level - Experimental Bomb
  • 5th Level - Volatile Savant
  • 9th Level - Corrosive Concoctions
  • 9th Level - Rapid Alchemy
  • 15th Level - Master of Ruin

3rd Level - Tool Proficiency​

When you adopt this specialization at 3rd level, you gain proficiency with alchemist’s supplies. If you already have this proficiency, you gain proficiency with another type of artisan’s tools of your choice.

3rd Level - Bombardier Spells​

You always have certain spells prepared after you reach particular levels in this class, as shown in the Bombardier Spells table. These spells count as artificer spells for you and don’t count against the number of artificer spells you prepare.

Artificer Level | Spells

  • 3rd | grease, inflict wounds
  • 5th | web, Melf's acid arrow
  • 9th | gaseous form, stinking cloud
  • 13th | greater invisibility, vitriolic sphere
  • 17th | wall of force, immolation

3rd Level - Experimental Bomb​

Starting at 3rd level, whenever you finish a long rest, you can magically produce an experimental bomb in an empty flask or vial you touch. Roll on the Experimental Bomb table to determine its effect, which is triggered when you throw it.

As an action, you or another creature can throw the bomb up to 30 feet, where it shatters and releases its effect. A creature in the bomb’s area must make a saving throw against your artificer spell save DC to avoid or reduce its effects.

You can create additional experimental bombs by expending a spell slot of 1st level or higher for each one. When you do so, you use your action to create the bomb and roll for its effect on the Experimental Bomb table.

Creating a bomb requires alchemist’s supplies on your person, and any bomb you create with this feature lasts until it is thrown or until the end of your next long rest.

Experimental Bomb Table​

  1. Corrosive Acid. Deals 2d6 acid damage in a 5-foot radius (Dex save for half). On a failed save, the acid clings to creatures, dealing 1d6 acid damage at the start of each of their turns until a creature uses its action to scrape or wash it off.
  2. Incendiary Fire. Deals 2d6 fire damage in a 5-foot radius (Dex save for half). On a failed save, creatures are set ablaze, taking 1d6 fire damage at the start of each of their turns until they or an ally uses an action to douse the flames.
  3. Toxic Gas. Creates a 10-foot radius cloud of poisonous gas that lasts for 1 minute. (Con save or be poisoned until the start of your next turn; repeat the save at the end of each turn). A creature must make the save whenever it enters the gas or starts its turn in the gas.
  4. Cryo Flask. Deals 2d6 cold damage in a 5-foot radius (Dex save for half). On a failed save, a creature is hindered by ice formations for 1 minute, or until it or another creature within reach uses an action to break the ice.
  5. Shrapnel Bomb. Deals 2d6 piercing damage in a 10-foot radius (Dex save for half). On a failed save, the creature takes 1d6 slashing damage the first time on each turn it moves 1 foot or more without teleporting. An affected creature or ally can use its action to remove the shrapnel.
  6. Sonic Blast. Deals 2d8 thunder damage in a 10-foot radius (Str save for half). Creatures that fail the save are pushed 10 feet from the center of the blast and are deafened until the start of your next turn.

Scaling: The initial and persistent damage increase at higher levels:

  • 6th level: +1d6 (or +1d4/+1d8 for relevant bombs)
  • 11th level: +2d6
  • 16th level: +3d6

5th Level - Volatile Savant​

At 5th level, you have mastered the art of combining spellwork and alchemical destruction into seamless attacks.

  • Whenever you cast an artificer spell or cantrip on your turn, you can throw one of your Experimental Bombs as a bonus action.

You can treat any alchemical item (such as alchemist’s fire, acid vial, or an experimental bomb) as a simple ranged weapon with a range of 30/90 feet, using your Dexterity or Strength modifier for attack rolls. Damage dealt by the throw is 1d4 slashing damage plus your Dexterity or Strength modifier, in addition to the damage or effect normally dealt by the item.

9th Level - Corrosive Concoctions​

At 9th level, your concoctions weaken enemy defenses and allow you to control the battlefield:

  • Creatures that fail their saving throw against one of your bombs have disadvantage on saving throws against the next alchemical effect you deal to them (from your bombs or alchemical items such as acid vial or alchemist’s fire) before the end of your next turn.

9th Level - Rapid Alchemy​

Also at 9th level, you are able to craft bombs with remarkable speed:

  • You can use a bonus action instead of an action to craft your Experimental Bombs.

15th Level - Master of Ruin​

At 15th level, your expertise in destruction allows you to maximize the potency of your creations and have better control over what you use:

  • You gain resistance to fire, and acid damage.
  • When a creature takes persistent damage from one of your Experimental Bombs (acid or fire) or an alchemical item (alchemist’s fire, acid vial), you can use your reaction to make the creature take maximum damage instead of rolling. You can use this ability a number of times equal to half your Intelligence modifier (minimum of 1). All expended uses are restored when you finish a long rest.
