Rook

Description​

Decisive tactics and thoughtful planning are both highly important factors in the game of chess, and as certain Bulwarks are aware, the same goes for the art of war. Rooks are master tacticians, able to turn the tide of battle in their favor from nearly any position.

Features:

  • 3rd Level - One Step Ahead
  • 7th Level - Recapture
  • 13th Level - Gambits
  • 18th Level - Checkmate

3rd Level - One Step Ahead​

When you select this specialization at 3rd level, you learn to apply yourself on the battlefield in a similar way to the pieces of a chess board.

You gain a pool of material equal to thrice your bulwark level to spend on plays. If a play allows you to move as part of it, you do so without expending your own movement, but you cannot have a speed of 0.

You recover all expended material when you finish a long rest.

Below is the list of plays:

Advance: When you take the attack action, you may expend three material to move up to your speed. You may take this movement before or after you make your attacks.

Back Rank: When you enter your defensive stance you can expend 3 material to impose disadvantage on all ranged attacks against you for the duration.

En Passant: When you attack a creature within 5 feet of you, you may expend 2 material to move up to 10 feet to another point within 5 feet of that creature. This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks, and your next attack roll against it has advantage.

Pawn’s Fury: During the first round of combat, you may expend 3 material to take an additional action. That action can be used to take only the Attack (one attack only), Dash, Disengage, Hide, or Interact with an Object action.

Knight’s Leap: When you jump, you may expend 1 material to triple your jump distance. It does not cost you any additional movement to do so.

Bishop’s Grace: As an action, you may expend 2 material to cast both guidance and resistance on yourself. Neither spell requires your concentration.

7th Level - Recapture​

Starting at 7th level, you learn to reclaim the potential of the foes you fell. When you kill a creature with at least 30 hit points, you recover 5 material.

13th Level - Gambits​

When you reach 13th level, you gain the ability to perform greater plays with great risk, though the potential payout is just as grand.

You gain access to the following plays:

Castling: When you enter your defensive stance, you may spend 7 material to choose one willing creature within 30 feet of you to trade places with. This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks. If when initiative ends, that creature has not taken damage since the switch, you regain 7 material.

Discovered Attack: When a creature within 5 feet of you targets you with an attack, you may use your reaction to expend 10 material. In doing so, you may make a melee attack against that target. If the attack reduces the creature to 0 hit points, you regain 10 material.

Promote: When you roll initiative, you may spend 20 material. For the duration of that combat you make plays, but not gambits, without expending material.

Queen’s Majesty: As a bonus action, you may expend 25 material in order to move up to twice your speed. For one minute after taking this bonus action, the amount of material you gain from your recapture feature is doubled.

King’s Command: As an action, you may spend 20 material to cast the Command spell (DC 8 + your strength modifier + your proficiency bonus) on any number of creatures within range. You must use the same command on each target. If no target succeeds their saving throw, you regain 10 material.

Rook’s Defiance: When you use your Fortitude feature, you may spend up to 20 material and further reduce the damage by that amount. If you reduce the damage to 0, you regain half the material spent.

18th Level - Checkmate​

At 18th level, you have mastered the art of bringing fights to a decisive finish. When you take the attack action while your stance is active, you may choose to end your stance early. If you do, the first creature you hit takes extra damage equal to your bulwark level and is knocked prone.
