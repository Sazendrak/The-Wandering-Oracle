Siren’s Crescent

Weapon (scimitar), very rare (requires attunement)

Attunement: Requires Attunement

Description

This finely crafted scimitar features a crescent-shaped blade adorned with intricate engravings. The blade is perforated with small holes and wind chambers, allowing it to emit a haunting, flute-like melody when swung or spun.

Harmonic Transformation: As a bonus action, you can transform the Siren’s Crescent into a musical instrument of your choice, provided you are proficient with that instrument. While using the transformed instrument, you have advantage on ability checks made to play it. You can revert the instrument back into the scimitar as a bonus action.

Arcane Harmony: If you can use a weapon as a spellcasting focus, the Siren’s Crescent grants a +1 bonus to spell attack rolls and to the saving throw DC of spells you cast through it.

Weapon Bonuses: You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls for melee weapon attacks made with this magic weapon.
