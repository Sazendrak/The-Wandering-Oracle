Thunderstrike Musket

Weapon (musket), rare

Attunement

Requires Attunement

Thunderstrike Musket

Description

This magical musket is imbued with the essence of thunder and has the following properties when attuned:

  • Thunderous Blast: On a critical hit, the weapon releases a powerful blast, dealing additional 2d6 thunder damage to the target and all creatures within a 10-foot radius of the target. The affected creatures must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be pushed back 10 feet.
  • Deafening Shot: Once per day, the weapon can be loaded with a Deafening Shot. The next time you make an attack with this weapon, on a hit, it unleashes a concussive blast that creates a 20-foot radius sphere of silence centered on the target, that functions similar to a silence spell for 1 minute and requires the shooter to hold concentration. The sphere moves with the target.
